Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

WorkdayHelper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information when you use our productivity platform at workerdayapp.tawonarwatida.co.zw.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Productivity data: Tasks, reminders, focus sessions, and chat messages you create within the app.
  • Google Calendar data: If you connect Google Calendar, we access your calendar events to provide scheduling features. We store OAuth tokens to maintain the connection.
  • Usage data: Analytics about how you use the app to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the WorkdayHelper service.
  • To generate AI-powered suggestions, day plans, and weekly reports using your task and calendar data.
  • To send health and task reminders you have configured.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. AI & Third-Party Services

WorkdayHelper uses the Groq API to power AI features (chat assistant, suggestions, day planner, weekly reports). When you use these features, relevant context (such as task titles and calendar event times) is sent to Groq's API for processing. No personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary is shared. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

5. Google Calendar Integration

If you connect Google Calendar, WorkdayHelper accesses your calendar events solely to provide scheduling and conflict detection features. We store your OAuth access and refresh tokens securely on our servers. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the Calendar page, which removes all stored tokens.

WorkdayHelper's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted on AWS (Amazon Web Services) in the EU North region. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and JWT-based authentication to protect your account. Passwords are hashed using BCrypt and never stored in plain text.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your tasks, reminders, and chat history from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.
  • Disconnect integrations: You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the Calendar page.

8. Cookies

WorkdayHelper does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage to store your authentication token and user preferences locally on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@tawonarwatida.co.zw
